Autobiography

My name is Beddoro Ayalew. Beddoro is a Hadiyan word which litterally means a person to rely on. I was born in Ethiopia in Chora, Buno-bedele Zone of Ormia region. I grew up in Bedele town. I attended my primary school thee in Bedele at Ras Tesema Nadew primary school, and Lemlem Karl Primary school. and I attended secondary school at Ingibi Secondary school and completed Grade 10 national exam with Great destniction scoring 10 As in each subject.

Regarding Preparatory School I attended Grade 11 at Bedele Preparatory school and the moved to town and completed secondary school there. I have scored 546 out of 600 on UEE and I joined school of Medicine

I have studied Medicine from 2017-2025 GC at Jimma university during my study I have served to attachments as student representatives and gained skills on leadership. I also won a Chinese government top scorers Prize. During internship I got A on 3 attachments. I have scored 86 out of 100 on National Exit Exam and passed COC assessment.

I had 2 months experience as from Oda Hulle General Hospital where I worked as General Practitioner.

Apart from medicine i am a tech enthusiast who is interested in Python programming and Front-End Web development. I have obtained digital badges and certifications from Cisco NetAcademy on Python programming, HTML, and AI.

Currently am workig as A General Practitioner and Lecturer at Mizan-Tepi university Teaching hospital.
